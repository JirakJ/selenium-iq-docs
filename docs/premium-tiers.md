# Premium Tiers

Choose the plan that matches your team's automation maturity. All tiers start with the Free plan baseline and can be upgraded without reinstalling the plugin.

| Tier | Ideal For | Key Capabilities | Pricing* |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | Solo testers exploring AI locators | Locator generation, limited self-heal credits, community support | $0 |
| Individual Professional | Freelancers or consultants | Unlimited AI locators, CI export, email support (48h) | $19/month or $190/year |
| Team | QA teams with 5-50 users | Shared knowledge base, analytics dashboard, Slack support (4h) | $99-$399/month based on seats |
| Enterprise | Regulated orgs with strict SLAs | SSO/RBAC, dedicated success engineer, 1h critical response | Custom (starting $499/month) |

> *Pricing reflects 2025 catalog. Contact sales for regional adjustments or volume discounts.

## Feature Comparison

- **Self-Heal Credits**: Free (50 per month), Individual (unlimited local, 500 CI), Team (unlimited local/CI), Enterprise (unlimited plus priority queue)
- **Locator Knowledge Base**: Available for Team+ with role-based permissions
- **Audit Log Export**: Enterprise-only, integrates with Splunk and Datadog
- **Custom Models**: Bring-your-own LLM endpoints in Enterprise tier

## Upgrading and Downgrading

1. Open IntelliJ → `SeleniumIQ` tool window → `Account` tab
2. Click `Manage Subscription`
3. Select the target tier and confirm billing cycle (monthly/annual)
4. Restart the IDE to refresh entitlements

Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Usage that exceeds the new tier limit is suspended until credits reset.
